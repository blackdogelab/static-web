How to Buy

BLACKDOGE is best purchased and sold through PancakeSwap, an ever-growing list of CEXs. Please note that, outside of PancakeSwap, exchanges which support one may not support the other.

BLACKDOGE is a decentralized experiment and, as such, we always incentivize the use of DEXs. If you choose to utilize a CEX instead, remember to research it first to ensure it is both safe and secure.

Quick Start Guide

Buy BLACK DOGE | BLDOGE

01
Create MetaMask wallet

Create a MetaMask Wallet using either a desktop computer or an iOS/Android mobile device. That will allow you to buy, sell, send, and receive BLACK DOGE.

02
Send BNB to your wallet

You can buy Binance Coin (BNB) directly on MetaMask or transfer it to your MetaMask Wallet from exchanges like Binance, etc. Make sure to use the BEP-20 network when transfering BNB.

03
Connect your wallet to PancakeSwap

Access your wallet to PancakeSwap by clicking ‘Connect to a wallet’ and selecting MetaMask.

04
Swap BNB for BLACKDOGE

You can start swapping as soon as you have BNB available! Press ‘Select a token’ and enter the token address or search for it on the tokens list.
